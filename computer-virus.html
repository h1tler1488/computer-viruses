What is a virus?

A computer virus is a program made to damage or access computers without permission. It can delete files, steal information, or slow down your system.

Types of viruses

  • File virus: Attaches to files and spreads when they are opened.
  • Boot virus: Infects the boot sector of the computer.
  • Macro virus: Uses macros in programs like Word or Excel.
  • Trojan: Pretends to be something useful but is harmful.
